When the gods have nightmares, it is Bane Elemental who brings them. Also known as Atropos, Bane was born from the midnight terrors of the goddess Nyctasha. A force of terror too powerful to be contained by sleep, he surfaced from her slumbers, fed upon her immortality, and stole his vaporous form from her inky blood. He is the essence of fear. Mortals who hear his voice hear their darkest secrets whispered in their ear. He calls to the hidden fear in every Hero's heart. Wakefulness is no protection, for Bane's black blood, continuously dripping, is a tar that traps his enemies in nightmare. In the presence of Bane, every Hero remembers to fear the dark.

Batrider is a ranged Intelligence hero who is strong at laning through applying debuff stacks, dealing heavy burst damage and zoning out his enemies. Sticky Napalm slows and amplifies his damage on enemies. This ability stacks, allowing strong lane control and Batrider to be played on any lane. His second skill Flamebreak can knock away enemies in an AOE while dealing burst damage. His Flaming Lasso ultimate at level 6 allows him to disable an enemy and drag them behind him. This combined with Firefly, which lays a trail of fire behind Batrider while giving him no-terrain collision, allows Batrider to constantly deal damage while pulling heroes out of position. One of the best items he can pick up is a Blink Dagger, allowing him to get in range and instantly initiate a fight with Flaming Lasso.

Clinkz is a mobile ranged Agility hero whose stealth and high damage arrows make him a large threat anywhere on the map. This hero is a bit squishy but has large burst damage and is able to run away easily. Clinkz's first skill is Strafe, an ability which allows him to greatly increase his attack speed. His ultimate Blood Pact allows him to consume a creep for an added percentage of Health and Damage. Skeleton Walk gives Clinkz a non-collision stealth after a brief fade time. This combined with the power of Searing Arrows, which gives extra damage versus both enemy units and buildings, makes Clinkz a deadly assassin. Lone junglers need to be afraid of Clinkz and teams need to focus on locking him down.
